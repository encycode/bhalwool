The Organisation

Bhal Nalkantha Khadi Gramodyog Mandal is a Khadi institution located in Ranpur, Botad District of Gujarat, India. This Institute's Vision is to provide employment to the local women by involving them into the work of spinning, handloom weaving, hand knitting, Dyeing and stitching of woolen khadi products.

Bhal Nalkantha Khadi Gramodyog Mandal was established by Ambubhai Shah, Harivallabh bhai Mehta, Dajibhai Dabhi, Chaturbhai Patel with the blessings of Jain Muni Shri Santbalji in 1958.

Production Process

Raw Material sourcing

Raw wool is sourced from Ludhiana. Cotton and Polyester materials are sourced from Ahmedabad.

Spinning

Raw wool is sent to the nearby villages where spinning is done by the local women.They came to the institution once a week to take raw material and submit the handspun yarns.

Yarn segregation

After spinning they segregate the yarns as per the quality. Yarns with more Knots and fluffiness send to the weaving department and finer yarns are send to the Knitting department.

Dyeing

They dye the yarns as per the color requirement. This step is optional because yarn dyeing is done if the required.

Warp preparation

They Fill the small Reels for the warp yarns from Kokda(Cone of yarns) Where they remove the knots and fluffiness (Foda) of the yarn. Then they give twist to the yarn and refill it in Reels with the use of Charkha.

Bobbin filling

The next step in the weaving department is The bobbin filling where they fill the bobbins for weft yarns which is called as weft preparation. They remove knotes and fluffiness of yarns and refill it with the use of Charkha.

Warping

For the warping they use Pawat(Frame with multiple reels for each yarn) and Beem for winding the warp yarn. Whole process is done by the women artisans. After setting the warps in Beem, They take it to the loom where they pass the warp from yield eyes(Galo) and Reed(Fani). They set the warps as per the design and requirement.

Weaving

After setting the warp and preparing the weft in bobbins they start weaving on the loom. They use Flying Shuttle for passing the weft (Vano). After passing the weft the beating is done with the use of Reed(Fani). In the similar way interlacement of warp and weft is done with the use of the Shafts.

Finishing

After the weaving they cut the extra threads on the surface of fabric to finish the fabric. This is done by the weaving artisans only. For the Shawls this fabrics goes to tie the tassels in the finishing department.

Dyeing

The ready fabric is gone for dyeing if the color is required. The shawls and fabrics are dyed in Acid dyes and it takes 1-2 days in this dyeing process.

Stitching

Dyed fabric is gone for stitching as per the product. The stitching of woven fabric is outsourced only. They give fabric for stitching in nearby villages.

Dispatch

After packing the products are send to the Khadi outlets and the order based products are send to their respective places.

Ironing

Stitched products are gone to the ironing department where they iron Products with Wet fabric and Tapping of Iron heat. Quality of the product is also checking at this level.

Packaging

Finally the Ready product is packed the bunch of five pieces or single pieces as per the product category. The Tags and Care labels are attached at the time of packaging.

Knitting Process

Raw Material sourcing

Raw wool is sourced from Ludhiana. Cotton and Polyester materials are sourced from Ahmedabad.

Spinning

Raw wool is sent to the nearby villages where spinning is done by the local women.They came to the institution once a week to take raw material and submit the handspun yarns.

Yarn segregation

After spinning they segregate the yarns as per the quality. Yarns with more Knots and fluffiness send to the weaving department and finer yarns are send to the Knitting department.

Dyeing

They dye the yarns as per the color requirement. This step is optional because yarn dyeing is done if the required.

Knitting Process

Wool cone Filling(Sutar Bharvu): They Fill the small Reels for the warp yarns from Kokda(Cone of yarns) Where they remove the knots and fluffiness(Foda) of the yarn. In this manner they prepare the yarns for the knitting and fill it in small cones or reels.

Knitting

The supervisor Vinodbhai set the yarns in the Manual hand knitting machine and pass it through the needles. After setting the yarns the women artisans start work of knitting on the Machine.Different products are made on different kind of machine.Sleeves of sweaters and body of sweaters made separately on the machine and then they went for stitching together.

Cutting & Stitching

Minimum required cutting is done by the cutting master and Stitching is done for the Product development by the women artisans

Ironing

Stitched products are gone to the ironing department where they iron Products with Wet fabric and Tapping of Iron heat. Quality of the product is also checking at this level.

Packaging

Finally the Ready product is packed the bunch of five pieces or single pieces as per the product category. The Tags and Care labels are attached at the time of packaging.

Dispatch

After packing the products are send to the Khadi outlets and the order based products are send to their respective places.
